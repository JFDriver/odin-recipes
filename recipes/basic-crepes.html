Basic Crepes

Description

Crepes are thin pancakes that are perfect for a weekend breakfast. They are easy to make and take a variety of toppings well, just like any pancake.

This recipe was stolen from all recipes. It is as simple, quick, and easy to make as it was to steal. These can be topped or filled with whatever you want. Toppings or fillings such as nut butters, Nutella, whip cream, jam, syrup, and fresh fruits all work well.

Equipment

  • Mixing Bowl
  • Ladle
  • Spatula
  • Griddle or Frying Pan

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup All-purpose Flour
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1/2 Cup Milk
  • 1/2 Cup Water
  • 1/4 tsp. Salt
  • 2 Tbs. Melted Butter

Steps

  1. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour and the eggs. Gradually add in the milk and water, stirring to combine. Add the salt and butter; beat until smooth.
  2. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium high heat. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each crepe. Tilt the pan with a circular motion so that the batter coats the surface evenly.
  3. Cook the crepe for about 2 minutes, until the bottom is light brown. Loosen with a spatula, turn and cook the other side. Serve hot.
