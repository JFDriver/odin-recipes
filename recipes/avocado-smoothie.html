Avocado Smoothie

Description

Avocado is a versatile fruit. Most people have seen it used in salsas and guacamole, in rice bowls or on toast. However, in some parts of the world, avocado is used as a dessert. This recipe is a perfect example. Hailing from Vietnam, the avocado smoothie is creamy, sweet, and delicious. Perfect for a hot day.

Equipment

  • A Blender

Ingredients

  • 2 Ripe Avocados
  • 2 1/2 Tbs. Condensed Milk (or to taste)
  • 1/2 Cup Milk
  • 1/4 Cup Ice

Steps

  1. Halve the avocados and remove the pit, then scoop the contents into a blender.
  2. Add the milk, condensed milk and ice into the blender along with the avocado.
  3. Blitz the contents on high until it has reached your desired consistency, then serve in a cup as is!

Tips

  • Slice the avocados and freeze them until firm. This works similarly to freezing bananas, giving the smoothie an added creaminess.
  • Use full cream milk. Other milk options will work with this drink, but for the most authentic and rich version, full cream is the to-go.
  • Don't go overboard on the ice. While it might be tempting to make the drink extra cold for a hot day, using too much ice will dilute the fruit flavor.
  • We like to use hass avocados because they're on the creamier side, which works well for this recipe.
