Egg Salad

Description

Egg salad is a simple dish that requires little in the way of ingredients or time to make. Japanese egg salad further refines this simplicity.

This recipe focuses on a few quality ingredients. It's perfect for an egg salad sandwich.

Ingredients

  • 3 large hardboiled eggs
  • 2 Tbps mayonaise
  • 2 tsp milk
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp sugar
  • 1/8 pepper

Steps

  1. In a medium sized bowl, mash the hardboiled eggs
  2. Add the rest of the ingredients and mix
  3. Put the egg salad in the fridge for about ten minutes for the flavors to meld
